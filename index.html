Georgia - September - 2025

Situated at the strategically important crossroads where Europe meets Asia, Georgia has a unique and ancient cultural heritage, and is famed for its traditions of hospitality and cuisine.

Nowadays Georgia has adopted its legislation to embrace the technological changes, and facilitating the access to banking system to entrepreneurs all over the world. Easy, accesible and remote operations enables people to find in Georgia an estrategic partner for world businesses.
